About Us

We created this website to showcase our story project, highlight the plot, and introduce the main characters. It serves as a creative space to share our ideas and present them visually.

Synopsis

A new romance between Reymark and Princes sends ripples through their social circle, exposing a profound, unfulfilled commitment from Reymark's past. As the emotional stakes rise, a dark revelation culminates in Reymark's untimely death. How many more secrets must be unearthed before the true nature of their bonds, and the killer, is revealed?
