About Us

A new romance between Reymark and Princes sends ripples through their social circle, exposing a profound, unfulfilled commitment from Reymark's past. As the emotional stakes rise, a dark revelation culminates in Reymark's untimely death. How many more secrets must be unearthed before the true nature of their bonds, and the killer, is revealed?

Character Infos

Reymark

A charming and easygoing young man, he finds himself caught between a blossoming new relationship and a significant promise from his past. His journey explores the complexities of love and the weight of forgotten vows.

Princes

A girl with a bright smile and an easygoing demeanor. She experiences the thrill of new affection, but her happiness triggers conflict within her closest friendships.

Rica

A loyal and deeply feeling individual, she has held onto a childhood promise with unwavering hope. Her world is shaken when that promise clashes with her best friend’s happiness.
